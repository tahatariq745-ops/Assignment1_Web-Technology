Our Story

Built by students, for students

Two university friends who couldn't find good affordable tech education — so they built it themselves.

🎯
Our Mission

To make world-class tech education affordable and accessible for every university student. We believe skills — not just degrees — open doors, and we're here to give students the ones that actually matter to employers.

👁️
Our Vision

By 2030, train 1 million students across South Asia and MENA and place them in jobs at the world's best companies. A world where talent, not privilege, determines opportunity.

Our Journey

How we got here

2021 — Founded
Started in a Dorm Room

Two CS students launch SkillSpark with 3 courses and 50 students. Zero funding, all passion.

2022 — Milestone
1,000 Students Enrolled

First bootcamp sees 87% job placement. $500K seed round. Team grows to 12 people.

2023 — Expansion
10 Countries

AI & Data Science track launched. 5,000 students. Series A of $3M closed.

2024 — Recognition
Best EdTech Startup

10,000 students. Google, Microsoft, AWS partnerships. Job Guarantee program launched.

2026 — Today
12,000+ Students

30+ countries, 48 courses, 200+ hiring partners. Targeting 100K students by 2027.
